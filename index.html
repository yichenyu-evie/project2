    Scottish Fold

    The first Scottish Fold cat was born at a farm in Scotland. The unique about this breed is that their ears folded forward and downward on their head and leave people a cute and special impression.

    American Curl

    Distinguished by truly unique ears that curl back in a graceful arc, American Curl has audiences in awe worldwide. Curls are very people-oriented, faithful, affectionate, and adapting to the new environment quickly with children, or other pets.

    British

    Comparing to other breeds, British cats are calmer when they become mature. They are easygoing in nature and talk infrequently. To be honest, British cats are relatively easier to keep for people who have never adopted a cat before.


  • Ragdoll British American Curl Scottish Fold Exotic Shorthair

    Ragdoll

    Ragdolls are large, laid-back, semi-longhaired cats with captivating blue eyes. They tend to be more interested in humans than others. They are known to run to greet you at the door, follow you from room to room, and generally choose to be where you are.

  • Exotic Shorthair Ragdoll British American Curl Scottish Fold

    Exotic Shorthair

    Exotic cats look similar to Persian but have short hair. They can usually be your quite, sweet, peaceful, and loyal companions. Sometimes, they quietly beg for your attention by just sitting in front of you with an irresistible look focused on your eyes.
